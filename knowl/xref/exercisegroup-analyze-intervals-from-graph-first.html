Exercise Group 5.12.26–29.

A function \(f\) is shown in Figure 5.12.1. Each question below is in reference to this function. Please note that the numeric endpoints of each interval answer are all integers.

26.

Over what intervals is \(\fd{f}\) positive and over what intervals is \(\fd{f}\) negative?
Answer.
\(\fd{f}\) positive over \(\ointerval{-\infty}{-8}\) and \(\ointerval{2}{\infty}\text{;}\) negative over \(\ointerval{-8}{2}\)
Solution.
\(\fd{f}\) is positive over \(\ointerval{-\infty}{-8}\) and \(\ointerval{2}{\infty}\text{.}\) \(\fd{f}\) is negative over \(\ointerval{-8}{2}\text{.}\)

27.

Over what intervals is \(\fd{f}\) increasing and over what intervals is \(\fd{f}\) decreasing?
Answer.
\(\fd{f}\) increasing over \(\ointerval{-3}{\infty}\text{;}\) decreasing over \(\ointerval{-\infty}{-3}\)
Solution.
\(\fd{f}\) is increasing over \(\ointerval{-3}{\infty}\text{.}\) \(\fd{f}\) is decreasing over \(\ointerval{-\infty}{-3}\text{.}\)

28.

Over what intervals is \(\sd{f}\) positive and over what intervals is \(\sd{f}\) negative?
Answer.
\(\sd{f}\) positive over \(\ointerval{-3}{\infty}\text{;}\) negative over \(\ointerval{-\infty}{-3}\)
Solution.
This is essentially the same exercise as the previous exercise. \(\sd{f}\) is positive over \(\ointerval{-3}{\infty}\text{.}\) \(\sd{f}\) is negative over \(\ointerval{-\infty}{-3}\text{.}\)

29.

The formula for \(f\) is \(\fe{f}{x}=\frac{x^3}{3}+3x^2-16x\text{.}\) Find the formulas for \(\fd{f}\) and \(\sd{f}\text{,}\) and then graph the derivatives and verify your answers to Exercises 5.12.26–29.
Answer.
\(\fe{\fd{f}}{x}=x^2+6x-16\text{,}\) \(\fe{\sd{f}}{x}=2x+6\)
Solution.
\begin{align*} \fe{f}{x}&=\frac{x^3}{3}+3x^2-16x\\ \fe{\fd{f}}{x}&=x^2+6x-16\\ \fe{\sd{f}}{x}&=2x+6 \end{align*}

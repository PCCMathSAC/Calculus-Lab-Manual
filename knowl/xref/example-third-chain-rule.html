Example 6.1.4.

\begin{align*} \text{Problem}&&&\text{Solution}\\ \text{Find }\lz{y}{x}\text{ if }y&=4^x\text{.}&\lz{y}{x}&=\fe{\ln}{4}\cdot4^x \end{align*}

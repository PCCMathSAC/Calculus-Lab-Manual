Find \(\lim_{x\to7}\left(4x^2+3\right)\text{.}\)
\begin{align*} \lim_{x\to7}\left(4x^2+3\right)&=\lim_{x\to7}\left(4x^2\right)+\lim_{x\to7}3&&\knowl{./knowl/xref/lla1.html}{\text{Theorem A.0.4}}\\ &=4\lim_{x\to7}x^2+\lim_{x\to7}3&&\knowl{./knowl/xref/lla3.html}{\text{Theorem A.0.6}}\\ &=4\left(\lim_{x\to7}x\right)^2+\lim_{x\to7}3&&\knowl{./knowl/xref/lla6.html}{\text{Theorem A.0.9}}\\ &=4\cdot7^2+3&&\knowl{./knowl/xref/llr1.html}{\text{Theorem A.0.1}}, \knowl{./knowl/xref/llr2.html}{\text{R2 A.0.2}}\\ &=199 \end{align*}

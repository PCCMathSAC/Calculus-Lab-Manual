Example 5.9.3.

\begin{align*} \text{Find }\fe{\fd{f}}{t}\text{ if }\fe{f}{t}&=\frac{4t^5-3t^3}{2t^2}\text{.}&\fe{f}{t}&=\frac{4t^5-3t^3}{2t^2}\\ &&&=\frac{4t^5}{2t^2}-\frac{3t^3}{2t^2}\\ &&&=2t^3-\frac{3}{2}t\\ &&\fe{\fd{f}}{t}&=6t^2-\frac{3}{2}\\ &&&=\frac{12t^2-3}{2} \end{align*}
Note that since \(f\) is undefined at \(0\text{,}\) \(\fd{f}\) is also undefined at \(0\text{.}\)

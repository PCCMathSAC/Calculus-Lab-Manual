Example 6.1.2.

\begin{align*} \text{Problem}&&&\text{Solution}\\ \text{Find }\lz{y}{x}\text{ if }y&=\fe{\sin}{x^2}\text{.}&\lz{y}{x}&=\fe{\cos}{x^2}\cdot\lzoo{x}{x^2}\\ &&&=\fe{\cos}{x^2}\cdot2x\\ &&&=2x\fe{\cos}{x^2} \end{align*}
The factor of \(\lzoo{x}{x^2}\) is called a chain rule factor.

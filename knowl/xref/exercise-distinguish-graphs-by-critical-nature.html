Exercise 2.

The curves in Figures 9.1.1 and Figure 9.1.2 were generated by two of the four functions given below. Use the given functions along with their first derivatives to determine which functions generated the curves. Please note that the \(y\)-scales have deliberately been omitted from the graphs and that different scales were used to generate the two graphs. Resist any temptation to use your calculator; use of your calculator totally obviates the point of the exercise.
The potential functions are
\begin{align*} \fe{f_1}{x}&=\frac{1}{(x-2)^{\sfrac{10}{7}}}+C_1&\fe{f_2}{x}&=\frac{1}{(x-2)^{\sfrac{2}{7}}}+C_2\\ \fe{f_3}{x}&=(x-2)^{\sfrac{2}{7}}+C_3&\fe{f_4}{x}&=(x-2)^{\sfrac{10}{7}}+C_4 \end{align*}
where \(C_1,C_2,C_3,C_4\) are unknown constants.

Example 5.9.2.

\begin{align*} \text{Find }\lz{y}{x}\text{ if }y&=\fe{\sec}{x}\fe{\cos}{x}\text{.}&y&=\fe{\sec}{x}\fe{\cos}{x}\\ &&&=\frac{1}{\fe{\cos}{x}}\fe{\cos}{x}\\ &&&=1\\ &&\lz{y}{x}&=0 \end{align*}
Note that since \(y\) is undefined for \(x=\frac{\pi}{2}+k\pi\) with \(k\in\mathbb{Z}\text{,}\) \(\lz{y}{x}\) is also undefined at such \(x\)-values.
